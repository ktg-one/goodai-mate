Strict Corner Radii (0-8px Sharpness)

Buttons and cards stay at 0-8px border radius. Inputs and tags are at 4px. Soft surfaces are 8px. Pill rounded corners (999px) are reserved purely for tags, badges, chips, and dots — never for buttons or container borders.

4px (--radius-xs) • Inputs, close keys
8px (--radius-sm) • Cards, chat, buttons
12px (--radius-md) • Legacy, deprecated
999px (--radius-pill) • Badge / Chip only

04. Copywriting & Voice

The voice is a switched-on Perth mate. We write like a reliable local tradie who happens to know systems inside out. Casual, direct, short sentences, warm, zero fluff. Use "we" instead of "I".

Forbidden Vocabulary

"AI", "Artificial Intelligence", "machine learning", "leverage", "synergy", "optimize", "streamline", "disrupt", "hyper-scale". Say "automation", "system", or "workflow" instead.

✅ Good'ai (Human Mate) ❌ Corporate AI Buzzwords

"Knock off early on Friday." "Optimize productivity outcomes."
