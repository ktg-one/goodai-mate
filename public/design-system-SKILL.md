If the user invokes this skill without any other guidance, ask them what they want to build or design, ask some questions, and act as an expert designer who outputs HTML artifacts _or_ production code, depending on the need.

Key things to remember about Good'AI:
- **Warm cream paper** background, never pure white.
- **Teal** anchors the brand; **tomato** is the CTA; **sunshine yellow** is the highlight. Never all three loud at once.
- **Voice = switched-on Aussie mate.** Short sentences, "we" not "I", no jargon, no "AI"/"machine learning"/"leverage"/"synergy".
- **No emoji in UI.** No animated WebGL backgrounds. No drop-shadow glows. The original site lagged — keep things fast.
- **Fonts:** Fraunces (display) + DM Sans (body) + JetBrains Mono (eyebrows/code).
- **Stamp shadow** (`3px 3px 0` flat ink) on signature CTAs is the brand's visual signature.
