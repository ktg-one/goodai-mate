Rules of the road

  • DOOne ink, one accent — pair a black or navy primitive with one accent primitive (gold/red). Two accents in close range is a yelling match.
  • DOFlat fills only. No gradients, no bevels, no drop-glows on the shapes.
  • DOTriangles point forward. Bars terminate. Halves bounce. The g — split disc + red half-disc smile — is the logo icon.
  • DON'TDon't rebuild the g mark out of these primitives, and don't promote a wordmark letter (the swan d, etc.) to standalone mark. The g is the icon.
  • DON'TDon't substitute a shape where a real icon belongs (status, action, navigation).
