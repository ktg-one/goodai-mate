Colours · Semantic tokens

Colours named by the job they do.

Instead of styling with a raw hex like #202C59, you reach for the role it plays — --bg-brand for a navy block, --bg-accent for the red CTA. Build everything against these role names and a rebrand becomes one edit in colors_and_type.css instead of a find-and-replace across every file. This card is the dictionary: token → colour → where it goes.
